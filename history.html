The History of George

George had a long and storied tale of his origin. Here are some of the highlights.

Dec. 6, 2002

The Birth of George

George was born to his parents Charles and Edna Boardman on December 6, 2002 at the Kaechon Poeple's Hospital in South Pyongyan. Charles and Edna decided on the middle name of Bernardus after their favorite TV series "Bernard". 

Aug. 16, 2017

Georgeman Begins his Culver Adventure

George's parents decided it was best for him to attend a military academy, so they decided upon the Culver Academies. George would later go on to recieve the rank of Corporal in the early 2019 school year.

Mar. 10, 2019

George Masters the Art of Latin

After recieving a perfect score on the National Latin Exam, George became nationally recognized for his Latin abilities. George considered the ability to fluently speak a dead language to be one of the greatest accomplishments of his life. When asked about it, George once replied with "Que esta pasando?"

Dec. 2, 2019

Sudden Death by Meat Grinder

George's sudden death by meat grinder shocked everyone in the Culver community, including George himself. George's death also brought much attention to how even simple household objects such as meat grinders can prove to be deadly when used improperly.
